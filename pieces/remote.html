Re-mote

by Filippo Santoro

This piece was written in 2013 for Marc Vallon. It is interactive in a different way than most interactive pieces for the bassoon. The audience members use their cell phones to vote on which sections of the piece will be performed and in what order. Then the bassoonist travels to different stands and plays the sections that were chosen. The author could not obtain the score for review to evaluate the difficulty of the bassoon part. The composer can be contacted at his website, which is currently down.
