Seismologic

by George Lewis

This piece was written in 2017 and was commissioned by Dana Jessen. The piece is undergoing an extensive revision and therefore could not be obtained for evaluation.

Recordings

There is a recording on the album The Recombinant Trilogy.

Difficulty

This piece is probably outside the ability of undergraduate students.

Style

The piece is in the avant-garde style and includes a section that is played on just the reed.
