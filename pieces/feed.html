Feed

by Ted Moore

This piece was written in 2018 in collaboration with Ben Roidl-Ward. The piece uses SuperCollider and some other hardware that makes it not as transferable to other performers. There is also no score, as it is a structured improvisation. The composer indicated that it may be possible to create a version that would be easier for other people to perform. The composer can be contacted at his website.
