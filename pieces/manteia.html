MANTEIA

by Peter Van Zandt Lane

MANTEIA is a piece with four movements called "Aeromancer," "Hydromancer," "Pyromancer," and "Chronomancer." Each movement has its own patch in Max/MSP. These pieces technically do not require a microphone or amplification of the bassoon part, though the patch allows for that if the performer desires. There is no signal processing in this patch, rather the patch triggers fixed media clips. This allows the performer to have more freedom than a standard fixed media piece.
