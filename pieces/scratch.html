Scratch

by Jean-François Charles

This piece was written in 2017 for a melodic instrument and electronics, and it was first performed by Benjamin Coelho. The piece uses an instrument called the Spectral DJ, which was built by the composer. As such, the only way to perform this piece is with the composer performing the electronics. The composer can be contacted at his website.
