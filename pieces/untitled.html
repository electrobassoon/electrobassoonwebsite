Untitled

by David Stenson

This piece was written in 2015. It features multiphonics, flutter-tonguing, and glissandi, but the durations are very long. Because of the slow changes, this piece could be done by advanced undergraduate students. The patch is undergoing extensive revisions, so further comment on the electronics is impossible. The composer can be contacted at his website.
