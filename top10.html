Recommended First Works

While the list of works section of this site is a good resource for finding repertoire, it may be useful to have a shortlist of good pieces for people the are newcomers to the genre. Here are some of my recommendations for pieces that are not too technically challenging, nor too active in the electronics requirements.

Effects Pedals

  • Things to Be and Other Miniatures for Amplified Bassoon and Effects
    by Mackenzie Jones
  • Black Lion by George Fetner
  • Fort Greene by Padma Newsome
  • Perfectly Logical by Tawnie Olson
  • Electrically Tragic by Gene Pritsker, Movement 2
  • Joyride Scherzo by Trevor Patricia Watkin

Computer-Based Processing

  • Shoot the Well by Clara Allison
  • Three Bagatelles for Bassoon and Electronics by Erich Barganier
  • Melodie for Bridget Piccirilli by Kyle Brown
  • Brown Velvet by Robert Carl
  • Gnar Kill by Benjamin Damann
  • Reaction, Reflection by Shuyu Lin
  • Ripped Up Maps by Andrew May
  • Mask by Stefano Scarani
  • Bassoonification by Dan VanHassel
  • Memory Shores by Bryan Wysocki

Of course, feel free to explore the other pieces as well! This is just meant as a starting point if the whole bibliography is too overwhelming!
