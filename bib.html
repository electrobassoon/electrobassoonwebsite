List of Works

Here is an alphabetical list of electronic works for the bassoon. They are grouped in two categories: pieces using effects pedals, and pieces using a computer. Each piece has its own page with detailed requirements for hardware, software, and performance ability. All pictures can be clicked to see an enlarged version. If you know of a piece that is missing from this list, please reach out to me through my contact page.
